1. Add 2 spoon besan into the vessel with Ghee and fry it till it become light
brown in color.

2. Add water accordingly (whatever amount is needed)

3. Add sugar in the pan and stirr the content till the Besan will leave the pan
and no more sticky is there.

4. Now if u want we can add some dry fruits in that too to make the taste better.

5. Serve the Halwa. :)
